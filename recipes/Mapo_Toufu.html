Mapo Toufu

Ingredient

  • 300g soft tofu (cut into cubes)
  • 150g ground pork or beef
  • 1 tbsp Doubanjiang (spicy fermented bean paste)
  • 1 tsp fermented black beans (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (minced)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns
  • Chili flakes or chili oil (to taste)
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (mixed with water)
  • 2 green onions (chopped, for garnish)
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Step

  1. Cut the tofu into cubes and soak in hot salted water for 5 minutes to firm it up. (≈5 min)
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add Sichuan peppercorns and stir-fry until fragrant, then remove them (optional). (≈1 min)
  3. Add minced garlic, ginger, and Doubanjiang. Stir-fry until the oil turns red. (≈2 min)
  4. Add ground meat and cook until browned and fragrant. (≈3–4 min)
  5. Add a splash of soy sauce, sugar, and fermented black beans (if using). Stir to combine. (≈1 min)
  6. Gently add the tofu cubes and a little water or broth. Simmer on low heat. (≈4–5 min)
  7. Add chili flakes or chili oil to taste. Stir gently to avoid breaking the tofu. (≈1 min)
  8. Mix cornstarch with water and pour it in to thicken the sauce. (≈1 min)
  9. Sprinkle chopped green onions on top and serve hot. (≈1 min)
