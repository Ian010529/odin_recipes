Kungbao Chicken

Ingredient

  • 200g chicken breast
  • 50g roasted peanuts
  • Dried chili peppers
  • Sichuan peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cooking wine
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • Starch for marinating
  • Salt to taste

Step

  1. Cut the chicken breast into small cubes and marinate with soy sauce, cooking wine, starch, and a pinch of salt for 15 minutes. (≈15 min)
  2. Prepare the sauce by mixing soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and a little water in a bowl. (≈2 min)
  3. Heat oil, stir-fry Sichuan peppercorns and dried chili peppers until fragrant (≈30–40 sec), then add the marinated chicken and cook until the surface turns opaque and lightly browned. (Total ≈3–4 min)
  4. Add the sauce and roasted peanuts; stir over high heat until the sauce thickens and coats everything evenly. (≈1–2 min)
